SECTION 1: DAKSHAA INFORMATION BLOCK DaKshaa is a national level
techno-cultural fest conducted annually. This section number is 1. The
fest includes technical, non-technical, and cultural activities.
Technical events include coding contests, hackathons, and project expos.
Workshops include AI, ML, Blockchain, IoT, Cybersecurity, and Full Stack
Development. Cultural events include singing, dancing, fashion shows,
and talent competitions. Participants register online through the
official website. Students from various colleges across India
participate in DaKshaa. The event enhances technical skills, leadership,
and communication abilities. Certificates and prizes are awarded to
winners and participants. The event promotes innovation, creativity, and
collaboration. Faculty coordinators and student teams manage the event
execution. Sponsors and industry experts may be invited for keynote
sessions. DaKshaa reflects the academic excellence of KSRCT.
